Model residuals — drag & SRP validation
What this validates (no GPS needed): For each consecutive TLE pair, the prior TLE is propagated forward to the next epoch using SGP4. The difference between that prediction and the actual next TLE's position/altitude reveals errors in the drag + radiation pressure model embedded in BSTAR. Large systematic residuals = the atmospheric density model or sail attitude is off.

BSTAR drag term trend
BSTAR encodes atmospheric drag at the TLE epoch. Increasing BSTAR over time = higher drag density (lower altitude or higher solar activity). This is the key sail performance indicator.
